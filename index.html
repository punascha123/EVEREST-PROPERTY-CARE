Weed Spray, Weed Killer & Weed Control Auckland

Weeds rob your lawn of nutrients, water, and beauty. Our professional weed spray service uses approved, targeted weed killers to eliminate dandelions, clover, paspalum, kikuyu, onehunga prickle weed, and other common Auckland lawn weeds — while keeping your grass healthy and green.

We use selective weed spray (kills weeds, not grass) for lawn treatment, and non-selective weed killer (glyphosate / Roundup-equivalent) for paths, driveways, fences, and garden beds where total vegetation control is needed.
